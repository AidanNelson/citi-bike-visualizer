Citi Bike Trips

This visualization represents my Citibike NYC bikeshare ride history from Oct 2016 - Oct 2017. Click on a station to see all routes originating at that station. Or, click here to see routes from a random station. Or, click here to show all routes at once.

Built using Mappa.js, P5.js and GraphHopper
